Shirong Zhao is an Associate Professor at the School of Finance, Dongbei University of Finance and Economics and a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charterholder. His primary research interests lie in financial institutions, efficiency and productivity, corporate finance and applied econometrics He received dual bachelor's degrees from Renmin University of China and Peking University, and earned his Ph.D. from Clemson University. His research has been published in internationally renowned journals such as the European Journal of Operational Research, Annals of Operations Research, Macroeconomic Dynamics, and Economics Letters. He has also served as the principal investigator for a National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) Youth Program project.
